Licensing Information

Transparency in our AI development processes

Ethical Data Sources

LyricVision is trained using carefully curated datasets that respect copyright and intellectual property rights. We source our training data from:

  • Public domain music and lyrics archives
  • Licensed content with explicit permission for AI training
  • User-contributed data with appropriate consent

Royalty-Free Output

Videos generated by LyricVision are:

  • Free from copyright claims on the visual elements
  • Safe for use on streaming platforms (note: original audio rights still apply)
  • Compliant with fair use guidelines

Our Ethical Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Regular audits of our training data
  • Removing any copyrighted content upon request
  • Continuous improvement of our ethical guidelines
  • Transparency in our AI training and development processes

For more detailed information about our data practices or to request removal of specific content from our training datasets, please contact our team.
